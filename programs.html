Arlington Chapter: LEGO Spike Prime (Grades 3-6)

Children will build robots using LEGO Spike Prime kits, learn basic programming concepts through visual interfaces, and develop problem-solving skills through fun challenges. No prior experience necessary; more advanced students may choose to pursue more creative explorations or be given extra credit challenges!

  • Free, community-run program in Arlington, MA
  • No prior experience needed
  • All materials provided
