Our Programs

Beginner Robotics (Ages 6-8)

An introduction to the exciting world of robotics! Children will build simple robots using kid-friendly kits, learn basic programming concepts through visual interfaces, and develop problem-solving skills through fun challenges.

  • 10-week program
  • No prior experience needed
  • All materials provided
Enroll Now

Intermediate Robotics (Ages 9-12)

Take robotics skills to the next level! Students will work with more advanced components, learn structured programming concepts, and complete projects that integrate sensors and motors to solve real-world problems.

  • 12-week program
  • Basic understanding of robotics recommended
  • Includes take-home project
Enroll Now

Advanced Robotics (Ages 13-16)

For serious young engineers! This program covers advanced programming concepts, electronics fundamentals, and complex mechanical design. Students will work in teams to design, build, and program robots for specific challenges.

  • 16-week program
  • Previous robotics experience required
  • Competition opportunities available
Enroll Now

Summer Robotics Camps

Immersive week-long camps during summer break! Each day combines structured learning with creative exploration. Students will complete a major robotics project by the end of the week.

  • Full-day and half-day options
  • Multiple age groups available
  • Showcase event for parents on final day
