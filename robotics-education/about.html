About RoboKids

Our Mission

At RoboKids, we believe that every child deserves the opportunity to explore the exciting world of robotics and coding. Our mission is to inspire the next generation of innovators, problem-solvers, and tech leaders through engaging, hands-on STEM education.

Our Story

Founded in 2015 by a group of passionate engineers and educators, RoboKids began with a simple idea: make robotics accessible and fun for children of all backgrounds. What started as weekend workshops in a community center has grown into a comprehensive educational program reaching hundreds of students each year.

Our Approach

We believe in learning by doing. Our curriculum combines structured lessons with open-ended exploration, allowing children to develop technical skills while fostering creativity and critical thinking. We maintain small class sizes to ensure personalized attention for each student.
